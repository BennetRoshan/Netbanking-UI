How to Stay Safe From Financial Fraudsters

Essential security tips to protect your wealth

BEWARE OF PHISHING LINKS!

Never click on suspicious links asking for your banking details.

USE STRONG PASSWORDS

Secure your accounts with unique, complex passwords and change them regularly.

BIG RETURNS, BIGGER RISKS

Verify investment schemes thoroughly before parting with your money.

NEVER SHARE YOUR OTP

Bank officials will NEVER ask for your OTP, PIN, or CVV over the phone.

VERIFY UNKNOWN CALLERS

Fraudsters pose as bank reps. Hang up and call the official bank number.

SAFE ONLINE SHOPPING

Only enter card details on secure websites starting with HTTPS.
